# Raw Idea: Smart Baseplate Sizing / Dimension Calculator

**Feature**: Smart Baseplate Sizing / Dimension Calculator

**Description**: Allow users to provide physical dimensions (width and length in mm) of a space they want to fill. The tool calculates how many gridfinity units fit in each direction (1 gridfinity unit = 42mm) and automatically creates a baseplate of that size.
